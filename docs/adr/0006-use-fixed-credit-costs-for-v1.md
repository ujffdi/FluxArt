# Use fixed credit costs for V1

FluxArt V1 will use a fixed credit cost table instead of dynamic pricing by model, size, queue, or style. The initial table is Text-to-Image at 10 credits per image, Image-to-Image at 15 credits per image, Inpainting at 20 credits per edit, Outpainting at 30 credits per edit, and HD no-watermark download at 5 credits, because fixed costs are easier for early users to understand and easier to audit while the product is still validating demand.
