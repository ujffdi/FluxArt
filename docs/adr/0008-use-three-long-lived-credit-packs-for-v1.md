# Use three long-lived credit packs for V1

Superseded by [ADR 0039](./0039-use-one-month-validity-for-new-credit-buckets.md).

FluxArt V1 will offer three Credit Packs: 500 credits for 29 CNY, 1500 credits for 79 CNY, and 5000 credits for 199 CNY. Purchased Credits from these packs should be long-lived, either effectively long-term or valid for at least two years, so early paid users do not feel pressured by short expiry while the product is still building trust.
